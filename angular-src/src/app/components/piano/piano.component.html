Piano Lessons

Master proper technique while learning your favorite songs


It's never too late to learn to play the piano. No matter your age or level of experience, Sphere's will customize the lessons to meet your musical aspirations.

Beginner

Students will learn how to read and write notes & rhythms, develop proper playing technique and learn basic music theory.
They will develop skills in sight reading and ear training, and have the opportunity to play their favourite songs and create some of their own.
Preparation for Royal Conservatory Exams at the beginner level is offered.

Intermediate

Students will expand on the basics learned in beginner lessons to include major keys, chords, and scales.
They will progress to more difficult levels of sight reading and ear training and move on to more challenging songs.
Preparation for Royal Conservatory Exams at the intermediate level is offered.

Advanced

The skills learned in the beginner and intermediate stages will be further expanded into playing more advanced music and understanding of music theory.
The new techniques learned here will provide fundamental building blocks for music composition and a deeper understanding of musical language that will allow more freedom in creative expression.
