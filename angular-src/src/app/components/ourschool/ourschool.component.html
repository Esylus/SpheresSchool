Our Purpose

Spheres strives to blend the worlds of classical music and electronic music production into a seamless education program that inspires and empowers students to explore, discover and express their passion for musical creativity and expression
After the fundamentals of musical language are learned students will be encouraged to create their own musical ideas. These musical ideas can be further developed into professional sounding songs using music production software such as Ableton Live or GarageBand

Meet The Team

Anna Kousk

Anna’s teaching philosophy arises from a space of inspiration and curiosity. In order to uncover the depths of each student’s unique form of creative self-expression, fun and enjoyment are fundamental to the process of musical learning, growth and exploration.  Anna has been playing piano for 23 years, composing and performing her own music for 10 years and teaching for 5 years. With a BAMus & Minor in Sonic Arts from the University of Calgary, and an Electronic Music Production certificate from Langara College she has developed  her own innovative teaching style. This style builds practical piano techniques and music theory as a basic foundation for understanding and exploring musical language. There are further opportunities to integrate this knowledge into music production using new music technology to create full professional sounding songs.

PJ Miller

PJ is an experienced composer, audio engineer and concert sound designer with over 10 years experience in the music industry. Blending his classical guitar roots with modern production and sound design techniques, he has sculpted his own compositional approach with intricate classically inspired melodies morphed by innovative sound design.  As a concert sound designer, he has tuned stereo rigs for concerts up to 20,000 people and has done many tours throughout North America and Europe. He spent several years as a sound designer in R&D at PK sound developing large-format line array loudspeakers and subwoofers. All this live sound design experience has left him with a unique approach to mixing and mastering studio music. He has been training audio engineers in the art of acoustics for over 6 years with many of his students going on to become touring sound technicians.
